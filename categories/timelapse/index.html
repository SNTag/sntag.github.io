DSLR timelapse

I’ve started a new project: using a Raspberry Pi to create a timelapse on a DSLR. I’ve recently moved to Binghamton NY, and from my 8th floor apartment, I have an amazing view of rolling hills. In the morning, clouds cover the summits. Preludes to rain drape the sky in rolling whites and greys. But I can’t communicate any of this to my parents back in Singapore, and while words could paint a picture, an actual picture is worth a 1000 words.
